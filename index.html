About Us

Anantham Is A Home For Senior Citizen Men & Women, which has 15 Self-Contained rooms for Senior Citizens. There are AC Rooms Available.

Every room has one Toilet, Solar System, Geyser. Every flat has a refrigerator. It has a hall, kitchen, air condition, office, visitor's room, swimming pool, 50,000 Sqft.

Garden Area, 24/7 Doctors, Nurse, Ambulance, Gym, Community Hall, Entertainment Hall, Temple Garden & Medical Centre & much more. Anantham is Centrally Located With Easy access to the Railway Station, Bus Stop, Market.

Services

Accomodation


Anantham has 15 self-contained room with attached toilet and bath facility. Each room is provided with a single bed, mattress, pillow and bed linen. Each room has two cupboards, a table and a chair.

Medical Facilities


Routinely medical check-up once a week with free medical services for minor illness. Nursing staff available 24 x 7.

Companion Care


For the companion care at Anantham, Professional staff and Local staff is available.

Picnics


We provide one day picnic every six months and one night stay once every year.

Meals


All the meals at Anantham are vegetarian. The menu of cosmopolitian variety and of all cuisines is served.

Security


We provide the safe and secure environment to all our residents. We have a dedicated staff for managing this. Cameras are installed at all critical locations.
